About Micrologix Nigeria Limited

Micrologix Nigeria Limited is a fast growing IT Service Provider with a passion for excellence. We are known to make the IT challenges of our customers our business.
Micrologix offers a wide range of services – from Hardware and Software solutions to Consultancy Services. Established in 2001, the company provides quality service to meet the ever changing demands of the industry. Micrologix has diversified business interests in IT Solution which can be customised to suite our various clients’ demands.
